<h1 align="center">VideoEngine</h1>

<h3>Notes</h3>

- This plugin is experimental. There may be an impact on server performance if a large number of players are watching a video at the same time.
- Videos are downscaled and can play at up to 20 FPS

<h3>Installation</h3>

1. Put VideoEngine.jar in your server’s plugins folder
2. Upload a video on  <a href="https://videoengine.netlify.app/">VideoEngine’s converter tool</a> and follow the instructions

<h3>Permissions</h3>

- ``videoengine.play`` - Play a video for yourself

- ``videoengine.stop`` - Stop a video for yourself

- ``videoengine.play.others`` - Play a video for yourself or other players

- ``videoengine.stop.others`` - Stop a video for yourself or other players
